Welcome to the City that never sleeps

There is so much to see in New York City! Its bustling streets are a whirlwind of activity, and there are world-famous sites around every corner. From the iconic landmarks like the Brooklyn Bridge to the city’s incredible food scene and the fascinating museums, you could spend months exploring NYC. Here’s my ultimate New York travel guide to help you plan an unforgettable trip!

Day trip itinerary

  1. 7:00am - Breakfast at Brookyln Diner

    It’s going to be a busy day so you’re going to need a lot of fuel. No better place for that the Brookyln Diner.

  2. 8:00am - Rockerfeller Center

    Get there early to beat the crowds and take a trip to Top of the Rock Observation Deck for the best views of New York.

  3. 10:30am - Walk (15min)

  4. 10:45am - Central Park

    After a short walk you will arrive at Central Park where you can take the sights of one of the worlds most spectacular metropolitan parklands. Don’t forget the famous fountain seen in the Friends intro.

  5. 12:30pm - Lunch at Earl’s Beer and Cheese

    Two streets east of Central Park is Earl’s Beer and Cheese where, as the name alludes to, you can get all kinds of grilled cheese creations and wash it down with a brew. Must try!

  6. 2:00pm - Museum Mile

    So much culture packed into such a small space with 7 museums in total. You won’t have time to visit all and if you only go to one then make it the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

  7. Evening - Uptown Harlem

    Spend the rest of the evening in the one of the America’s most vibrant neighborhoods. Harlem is the capital of African-American history and culture. If you’re there on a Wednesday check out the Apollo Theater’s Amatuer Night.
